About Us

Z4B Studios is an independent game studio with a passion for storytelling through play.

Our goal, is to create games with deep stories, seamlessly blended into fun, immersive gameplay.

We are aiming to craft story-rich games, where players uncover hidden truths through exploration, interaction, and the choices they make.

SIGK111

A terminal-driven puzzle game about trapped voices, forgotten machines, and the lines between human and code.

You’re a hacker. Drifting the web. Until you find a signal — buried, encrypted, abandoned.
A sealed facility. Buried on purpose. Lost for a decade.

You wonder what secrets it keeps...
Then it types back.

“Who is it?”

Someone is alive inside.

But...
How did they end up sealed in there?
How have they survived this long?
How is the network even still active?

Whoever — or whatever — it is...

Thymos

A dystopian, post-apocalyptic soulslike set in the haunting world of Dolmora — a realm once alive with soul and spirit, now reduced to a lifeless, mechanical husk.

Scarred by the sins of its past, Dolmora echoes with the hollow rhythms of hearts trapped between life and oblivion.

Its inhabitants, stripped of their humanity, now exist in cold robotic shells. In this world, redemption isn’t spiritual — it’s survival, a desperate reclamation of what it means to be alive.

Only hearts remain as the final tether to what was once human.

Thymos is a morally complex Metroidvania journey through this broken world.

You play as HIM07 (Heart-Implemented Machine, Generation 7) — or Romih, as the sword calls him.

Awakening in a dark pit beside the sentient blade Heartforge, you have no memories. Only questions. Who are you? What happened to this world?

Fight, explore, and rebuild your identity in a realm ruled by metal, memory, love, and loss.
